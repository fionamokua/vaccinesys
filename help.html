Help Page

1. If you are a new user, click register to open the registration form.

2. All inputs with * are required.

3. Your identification is very important, ensure you enter the right details.

4. Ensure you fill in the correct contact details as they will be used to send your next vaccination schedule message.

5. Your next of kin details are required.

6. Select your location of residence i.e where you live.

7. Select the allergies/preexisting conditions that you have. If they are not in the list, fill in using the other allergies/conditions option.

8. Confirm your details then click submit to complete registration.
